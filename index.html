Privacy Policy

Fork This Dragon  ·  Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fork This Dragon ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account information: Username and email address when you register or sign in via Google.
  • Game data: Your player level, experience points, gold, equipment, dragon kill count, and other in-game statistics.
  • Activity data: Actions taken in the game such as combat results, items purchased, and messages posted to the in-game chat.
  • Device information: Basic device identifiers used for ad serving and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your game account and save your progress.
  • Display your username and stats on the in-game leaderboard.
  • Enable in-game social features such as the chat channel and direct messages.
  • Serve advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Improve the game experience and fix technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Supabase — our backend database provider. Your account and game data is stored securely on Supabase servers. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In — used for optional account authentication. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — used to serve in-app advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers listed above, to the extent necessary to operate the App.
  • If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Leaderboard & Public Information

Your username, player level, and dragon kill count are displayed publicly on the in-game leaderboard. Messages you post in the in-game Slack channel are visible to all players. Please do not share personal information in public chat.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and game data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including server-side validation of all game actions and secure storage via Supabase. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. You can also opt out of personalised advertising through your device's advertising settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
